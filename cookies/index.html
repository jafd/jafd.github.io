We value your privacy

In fact, I value your privacy so much that I don’t use cookies or a similar storage technology on this website at all.

Your data is safe with me, because I simply don’t collect it.

I’m not interested in seeing how many people have seen these pages. I don’t care about engagement metrics, and I don’t sell ads.

Mind you, however, that this site is being hosted by Github. They do collect your IP address each time a page from this site is served. They also don’t specify for how long they retain it. If you need to be anonymous with them, use a proxy or somesuch. Also, complain to them.
